2.1 Price-based regime features (pre-earnings context)
Computed as-of each day, shifted safely.

Trend / Drift

drift_60d — slow regime bias
drift_20d — medium bias
(optional) drift_5d — short-term sentiment

Volatility

vol_10d — short-term tension
vol_30d — baseline volatility
vol_ratio_10_30 = vol_10d / vol_30d

Momentum

mom_5d — earnings run-up / selloff
mom_20d — medium narrative momentum

2.2 Earnings-specific setup features (core to Breakwater)

days_to_earnings
is_earnings_week (≤5 days)
is_earnings_window (≤10 days)

Historical reaction

abs_reaction_median_3d
abs_reaction_p75_3d
directional_bias (mean signed reaction)

Consistency

reaction_std
reaction_entropy (chaotic vs predictable mover)

These alone justify the product.

2.3 Cross-sectional context (relative risk)
Risk is relative, not absolute.

sector_vol_30d
stock_vs_sector_vol = vol_30d / sector_vol_30d
sector_earnings_density
(% of sector reporting this week)

2.4 Structural risk features
These explain why a move might be violent.

beta (yes, but low weight)
market_cap_bucket
liquidity_proxy (avg dollar volume)
gap_frequency (how often it gaps > X%)
